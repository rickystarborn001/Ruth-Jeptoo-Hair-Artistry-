Story

Hello, I'm Ruth Jeptoo, an enthusiastic hairdresser, completely entranced by the world of hair. This is my ambitious journey, fueled by love and a hunger for excellence. From classic hairstyles to unique crafts, with every client, I strive to transform hair into an art form that reflects their unique style and confidence. My passion propels me to perfect my skills and embrace new trends. I believe that through hair, we can empower individuals, unlocking their inner beauty and bolstering their self-assurance. Experience the ambition firsthand. Elevate your hair journey with me. Let's create beauty that resonates and stories that inspire. Welcome to my world of ambitious hair artistry!

WHAT I DO !!!

01. Bantu Knots

Step into a world where heritage and trend converge with "Bantu Knots." This iconic hairstyle pays homage to its African roots while embracing contemporary style. It highlights the beauty of diversity and honors the legacy of a timeless tradition.

02. Knotless Braids

Introducing "Knotless Braids," a manifestation of refined beauty and comfort. These braids transcend traditional methods, seamlessly starting at the roots for a gentle, tension-free experience. A fusion of style and ease.

03. Bridal Hairstyles

From intricate updos to flowing waves, I craft enchanting looks that enhance your bridal glow. Each hairstyle captures the essence of romance and sophistication, ensuring you shine on your special day.
